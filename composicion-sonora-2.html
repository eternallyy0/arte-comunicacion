Composición Sonora

Link: Audio

Transcripcion: 

My fellow citizens.
Justice. Freedom. Blonde. Revolution. Fabulous.
I stand before you. Supercalifragilistic, no, wait, wrong show

America, tonight, I bring heaven and hell,
pineapple and murder,
when the glitter fades, blood, donkey, bling bling bling.

The future is green,
the anthem is blue,
The economy is money money money!

I do not fear, because tonight we are all Rocket Men,
we are the Cyclone,
We are one of the clowns.

Someone in the crowd says: Shut up
I say love: hakuna matata
They cry: we'll be here
I answer: is another day of sun

Children of tomorrow: Raindrops on roses
Bow down to no one, except maybe me
Pretend you know what you're doing 

We must march together: One short day
We must dream louder: My shot
We must believe harder: Don’t dream it, be it

So come tonight, my nation,
let us rise up with you disco fever,
let us shine with a glorious purpose,
let us dance in time warp.

And when history wonders what we were
say: Legally Blonde,
say: Cabaret,
say: Tomorrow belongs to me
